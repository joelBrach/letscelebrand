OVERVIEW

In 2014, Celebrand was conceived by artist and branding genius Joel Schwartz, with a clear mission: To provide an opportunity to businesses and professionals, who are seeking to build a solid customer-oriented brand and forge a long-lasting audience within the ever-growing Orthodox Jewish consumer base, in a highly professional manner not commonplace in the community. Ever since, Celebrand has been providing solutions to end-user clients seeking to be Celebranded℠, as well as marketing agencies whose clients saw a growing need for great branding.

Therefore, a word of caution: If your idea of branding is “just make me a logo and business cards” and that’s what you’re after, then hiring us would be a waste of time.
